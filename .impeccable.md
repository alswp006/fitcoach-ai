```markdown
# FitCoach AI — Design Context

## Users
**Personas:** 20~40대 홈트레이닝 입문자 (직장인, 주부, MZ세대) who need real-time form correction but can't afford ₩500k+/month PT sessions or don't want generic YouTube guidance. They use Toss ecosystem daily and expect mobile-first, frictionless experiences. **Job to be done:** Get immediate, personalized feedback on exercise form to build confidence and prevent injury at home.

## Brand Personality
**Personality:** Encouraging, Precise, Approachable. **Tone:** Supportive expert — motivate progress ("great form on that last rep") while delivering clear corrections ("left knee is turning inward; adjust your stance"). Conversational and warm, never condescending. Align with Toss's "Simple. Efficient. Trustworthy" positioning.

## Aesthetic Direction
TDS (Toss Design System) 준수—@toss/tds-mobile 컴포넌트 전용. 커스텀 미적 방향 없음. 모든 UI는 TDS 토큰(색상, 타이포그래피, 스페이싱, 애니메이션)을 따릅니다.

## UX Principles
1. **Real-time responsiveness.** Video stream and pose feedback must have <200ms latency; users need immediate, trustable corrections during active movement.
2. **Clarity over comprehensiveness.** Surface one actionable correction at a time in plain Korean; bury detailed metrics (joint angles, muscle activation) in post-workout reports, not live UI.
3. **Psychological safety.** Position AI feedback as coaching, not judgment. Use encouraging language and celebrate small wins; privacy controls for camera must be transparent and prominent.
4. **Progressive disclosure.** Beginners see basic form cues and rep count; power users unlock detailed analysis, custom plan adjustments, and challenge leaderboards.
5. **Confidence through evidence.** Show before/after video clips and weekly progress charts so users internalize improvement—motivation sustains retention better than gamification alone.
```
